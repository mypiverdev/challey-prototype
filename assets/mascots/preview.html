Design System Notes

  • Consistent style: Rounded shapes, large expressive eyes with dual highlights, pink cheek blush, friendly proportions
  • Brand integration: Each mascot wears a unique Challey-branded accessory (bandana, collar, scarf, hat, bow tie, medal, crown, star badge, bamboo star, winter hat, checkmark crown, party hat)
  • Color palette: Each animal has its own natural colors, with Challey brand blue (#2E86AB) and orange (#F18F01) appearing in accessories
  • Scalable SVG: All 400x400 viewBox, works from 32px avatar to full-screen display
  • Character personality: Each mascot's personality maps to a chore/reward behavior (collector, leader, streak-keeper, etc.)
